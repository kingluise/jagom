Welcome to JAGOM

Welcome Home

Dear Brothers and Sisters in Christ,
Greetings in the precious name of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ!
Welcome to JESUS ARMY GLOBAL OUTREACH MINISTRIES. Our church is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the Gospel and living out the teachings of Jesus.
We believe in the transformative power of God’s love and grace. Our mission is to share the message of hope, healing, and salvation found in Christ. We want you to feel a part of our family, embraced by the love and fellowship that binds us together.
As we gather to worship and seek God’s presence, let us open our hearts to His guidance. Together, we can make a significant impact in our communities, shining the light of Christ in a world that needs it.
Thank you for being here. May you find encouragement, inspiration, and a deeper connection with God as you participate in our services and activities.
May the grace and peace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you always.
In His Service,

PASTOR SUNDAY OYELEYE
General Overseer
